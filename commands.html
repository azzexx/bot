Music

Dai join

Azexx enters your voice channel.

Dai play

Joins your voice channel and starts playing 24/7.

Dai stop

Leaves the voice channel.

Dai skip

Skip the currently playing song.

Dai song

Shows the current playing song.

Dai loop

Toggle music loop.

Dai pause

Pause the currently playing music.

Dai resume

Resume currently playing music.

Dai volume

Check or change the current volume.

Dai station

Switches between radio themes.

Dai disconnect

Leave the current channel.
